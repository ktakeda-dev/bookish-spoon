# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 27, 2026**

## Overview

This privacy policy describes how **myTE Working Hours Input** ("the Extension") handles user data. The Extension is developed by Kosuke Takeda.

## Data Collection

The Extension **does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data**. No information is gathered from users of this Extension.

Specifically, the Extension does NOT collect:

- Personal identification information
- Usage or telemetry data
- Cookies or tracking information
- Location data
- Any other form of user data

## Third-Party Services

The Extension does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services for analytics, advertising, or any other purpose.

## Data Storage

Since no data is collected, no data is stored on any servers or external locations. All operations performed by the Extension are processed locally on the user's device.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
